Post-treatment Individual Tumor Volume Assessment2

Individual tumor response was assessed in 93 patients (77.5%) with post-treatment assessments*

  • 63.3% (n = 59/93) had a decrease in tumor volume

  • 36.6% (n = 34/93) patients had an increase in tumor volume

Assessments were based on the difference in the sum of the products of greatest diameters between baseline and post-treatment assessments. No statistical inferences can be drawn from these results

*93 of the 120 patients had measurable disease at both baseline and post-treatment assessment.
