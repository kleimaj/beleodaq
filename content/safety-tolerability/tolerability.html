SAFETY TOLERABILITY

Tolerability Established With the BELIEF Trial1,2

Most patients tolerated the target dose of BELEODAQ1,2

12% OF PATIENTS receiving BELEODAQ required a dosage reduction due to AEs

88% OF PATIENTS receiving BELEODAQ did not require a dosage reduction due to AEs

In studies, BELEODAQ has not been associated with clinically relevant changes in heart rate or PR- or QRS-interval duration2

Dose reductions due to prolonged QTc or increased transaminases occurred in 2 of 129 patients1,2

Independent central review of ECG data identified these 2 patients with grade 3 QT prolongation and concluded that BELEODAQ had no effect on cardiac repolarization2

Pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic analyses showed no correlation between BELEODAQ concentration and changes in QTc from baseline2

BELEODAQ is the only agent that the FDA has approved for the treatment of R/R PTCL that was studied in patients (N = 120) with platelet counts down to 50,000/μL2

  • R/R PTCL patients with platelet counts <100,000/μL are typically ineligible for clinical trials10
