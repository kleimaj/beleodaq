TRIAL DESIGN BASELINE CHARACTERISTICS

BELEODAQ Is for Adult Patients With PTCL at First or Subsequent Relapse1

The BELIEF trial evaluated the ORR* with BELEODAQ, a single-agent treatment regimen1,2

  • BELIEF was a single-arm, open-label, nonrandomized, international phase 2 study of 129 adult patients with R/R PTCL1,2

  • 120 of the 129 had histologically confirmed PTCL and were evaluable for efficacy1

  • The median time from PTCL diagnosis was 12.0 months (range, 2.6-266.4)2

Select Inclusion Criteria2

  • Aged ≥18 years

  • PTCL by histologic confirmation

  • At least 1 prior systemic therapy

  • Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group (ECOG) Performance Status score 0-2

  • ANC ≥1000/μL

  • Platelets ≥50,000/μL

*In the BELIEF trial, ORR included patients with a complete response and with a partial response.1,2

AE, adverse event; ANC, absolute neutrophil count; BMB, bone marrow biopsies; CR, complete response; ECOG, Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group; HSCT, hematopoietic stem cell transplantation; IRC, independent review committee; IWG, International Working Group; ORR, overall response rate; PD, progressive disease; PR, partial response; PTCL, peripheral T-cell lymphoma; R/R relapsed/refractory; SAP, statistical analysis plan.
