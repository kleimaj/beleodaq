TRIAL DESIGN BASELINE CHARACTERISTICS

BELEODAQ Is for Adult Patients With PTCL at First or Subsequent Relapse1

The BELIEF trial evaluated the overall response rate (ORR*) with BELEODAQ, a single-agent treatment regimen1,2

  • BELIEF was a single-arm, open-label, nonrandomized, international phase 2 study of 129 adult patients with R/R PTCL1,2

  • 120 of the 129 had histologically confirmed PTCL and were evaluable for efficacy1

  • The median time from PTCL diagnosis was 12.0 months (range, 2.6-266.4)2

1000 mg/m2 by intravenous infusion over 30 minutes once daily on days 1-5 of a 21-day cycle2

  • Two dose reductions for hematologic and nonhematologic toxicity were permitted in 25% increments, after which BELEODAQ was discontinued

  • Cycles were repeated every 3 weeks until PD, death, unacceptable toxicity, HSCT, loss to follow-up, or patient or investigator decision

*In the BELIEF trial, ORR included patients with a complete response and with a partial response.1,2
