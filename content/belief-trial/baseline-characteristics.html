TRIAL DESIGN BASELINE CHARACTERISTICS

BELIEF Included Patients With R/R PTCL Who Were Representative of a Broad PTCL Patient Population

BELEODAQ was studied across a range of PTCL subtypes1

PTCL subtypes studied1

(N = 120, based on central diagnosis)

PTCL-NOS, AITL, and ALCL are the 3 most common subtypes of PTCL9

The median age was 64 years (range, 29-81)1,2

  • 48% of patients (n = 62) ≥ 65 years1

  • 10% of patients (n = 13) ≥ 75 years1

Patients were treated with BELEODAQ after failing at least 1 prior systemic therapy2,*

  • Patients received a median of 2 prior treatments (range 1-8)

  • Some patients were heavily pretreated with up to 8 prior treatments

21% of patients (n = 25) underwent an HSCT before entering the BELIEF trial2

The BELIEF trial included patients with baseline platelet counts between ≥50,000/μL and < 100,000/μL2

*Prior systemic regimens (N = 120) included 97% CHOP or CHOP-like regimens, 68% other multiagent regimens, 25% single-agent regimens, and 3% corticosteroids2

AITL, angioimmunoblastic T-cell lymphoma; ALCL, anaplastic large cell lymphoma; CHOP, cyclophosphamide, doxorubicin hydrochloride (hydroxydaunorubicin), vincristine sulfate, and prednisone; HSCT, hematopoietic stem cell transplantation; PTCL, peripheral T-cell lymphoma; R/R relapsed/refractory; PTCL-NOS, PTCL not otherwise speciﬁed.
