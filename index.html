Indications and Usage

BELEODAQ is a histone deacetylase inhibitor indicated for the treatment of adult patients with relapsed or refractory (R/R) peripheral T-cell lymphoma (PTCL). This indication is approved under accelerated approval based on tumor response rate and duration of response. An improvement in survival or disease-related symptoms has not been established. Continued approval for this indication may be contingent upon verification and description of clinical benefit in the confirmatory trial.
